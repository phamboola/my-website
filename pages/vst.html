Volvo Studio
Tokyo Explorer

We were approached with a task of inventing an AR experience that could showcase the latest features of Volvo’s new line of electric cars in a creative and playful way to appeal Volvo as a brand towards Japanese Millenials and Gen-Z. The experience would showcase upon the reopening of the new Volvo Tokyo flagship showroom in Aoyama Tokyo.

The problem?
Young people just aren’t interested in buying a car.

Concepting the visuals
for the northern lights

We gathered moodboards of any relevant visualisations that would look beautiful in the studio space - we particularly looked at procedural waveform art. One of our concepts took the form of the Northern lights as inspiration for the AR space and experimented with artistic visualisations similar to nordic art, soundwaves and chalk drawings.

Aurora Borealis AR concepts

We knew from the broad consensus amongst younger demographics a desire to explore and appreciate culture so we set our sights on bringing the sights you could only see in Sweden, down to the Volvo Tokyo Studio
