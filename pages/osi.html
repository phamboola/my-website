Healthy Eating through Play

Our Special Island is a play-based app available on the app store. It was initially released as a health initiative study in Tonga - introducing healthy eating concepts and habits through play.

I worked as the Lead Designer for this - developing wireframes and keyflows, finished art and graphic design to assist in the campaign.

The app has won awards for Healthy Eating in the Pacific Region and for visual design and storytelling.

After the initiative, a 81% uplift in children in Tonga were able to recognise healthy foods while 60% increase of kids chose healthy eating after the initiative.
