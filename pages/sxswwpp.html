Where Art meets Commerce

Merging Realities - an online AR tasting experience to showcase the power of Web3 and AR technology.

Sketch concepts done in Illustrator. My initial thinking was how to develop an interesting out of norm graphic design piece that could be translated to interface design.

Alpha design concept and moodboards featuring vaporwave styletones. I was particularly inspired by vaporware design and planetary tattoo design.

I worked systematically to develop a design system for the experience. Starting atomically and then dripping down to larger assets and patterns. The addition of more neon floral colours and chromatic 3D assets amplified the visual style.

As the lead visual artist, I also created the 3D model for the NFT artwork for the event in Blender. I had a fun time learning shaders and crafting my own maps that also work in 8thwall AR.

Entry screen designs including creating an account and setup through email campaign

Visitors can sign up for the Web3 session and review their minted 3D NFT artwork and engage in future content releases.
