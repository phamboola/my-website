AustralianSuper
App

I started this work as part of a team of 2 Designers, 2 Developers and a Product Manager initially in 2017.

We worked with AustralianSuper to deliver a MVP which included onboarding, transactions, membercards and BPAY contributions and continued on to push out releases in the following years on as part of the BAU lineup.

I worked primarily on developing the homescreen and BPAY contribution which included micro animations to signify feedback events.

Examples of BAU features included Member Direct access in the app itself instead of directing to the website portal.

Eventually the brand refresh came and I implemented the design language across to the system design in the app. We migrated from Sketch and Invision over to Figma and started afresh.

Around this time I was the sole Designer working on this alongside the client's internal lead Service Designer. We developed a great relationship and pushed out the designs in the brand refresh and included new features such as the new Dashboard with performance overview as well as stronger security features such as FaceID and Fingerprint.
