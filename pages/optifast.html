Optifast App

I worked on the Optifast app to develop icons and help out with UI Design across the board. I was in a team of 3, being a junior design and working under the Design Director.

I concepted the app icon for the experience as well as the onboarding icons. We took inspiration from video game badges and applied those principles here.

I helped work on the card designs as well for the home sections, balancing spacing and icons as well as typography and colours to ensure visibility.

Using gamification principles and fun designs, we wanted to be engaging visually but be engaging interactively - allowing for playful designs to pop through the clean look and navigating health spaces.
